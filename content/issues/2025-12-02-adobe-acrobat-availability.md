*Issue Resolved | 02:38am Central* - Adobe reports that the issue is now resolved. The Atelic Technology Services Department appreciates your patience and understanding while working through this service outage. If the issue is still occurring, please contact the Technology Services Department. This will be the last update unless the issue recurs.

*Report Received | 01:39am Central* - We are investigating a potential issue that might affect the uptime of one of our services. Adobe reports that users are unable to access Adobe Document Cloud storage, search within Adobe Document Cloud, manage user subscriptions, and convert PDFs within Adobe Acrobat. The Atelic Technology Services Department has not been able to validate this report and are monitoring further updates from Adobe. End user disruption may occur for this issue.
