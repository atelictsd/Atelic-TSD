*Issue Resolved | 03:36pm Central* - The Technology Services Department reports that the issue is now resolved. The Atelic Technology Services Department appreciates your patience and understanding while working through this service outage. If the issue is still occurring, please contact the Technology Services Department. We have confirmed that connectivity has been restored on our FortiGate management dashboard.
